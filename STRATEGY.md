## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**Risk**: LLM output quality varies
- **Mitigation**: Structured prompt engineering, validation, human review

**Risk**: API costs become prohibitive
- **Mitigation**: Model optimization, caching, customer tier-based limits

**Risk**: LLM API outages
- **Mitigation**: Multi-provider strategy (OpenAI + Anthropic), graceful degradation

### Market Risks

**Risk**: ChatGPT adds structured planning feature
- **Mitigation**: Focus on enterprise, specialization, better UX

**Risk**: Low willingness to pay
- **Mitigation**: Free tier generates demand, upsell on value, team plans

**Risk**: Competitor launches similar product
- **Mitigation**: First-mover advantage, community moat, integrations

### Product Risks

**Risk**: Users don't follow generated plans
- **Mitigation**: Progress tracking, accountability features, community support

**Risk**: Low engagement/retention
- **Mitigation**: Onboarding, email reminders, iterative improvements
